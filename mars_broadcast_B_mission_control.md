# MARS BROADCAST — VIDEO B · MISSION CONTROL (Houston)
### One of two intercut videos · 20 clips × ~10s · English

This is the **Houston side** of the broadcast: a single flight controller at his console, watching the Mars feed on the monitor in front of him. **This file contains ONLY Mission Control's voice and actions.** The astronaut's voice and the Mars visuals live in **VIDEO A** and get cut in during editing.

His arc is the whole engine of the comedy: he starts calm and professional, slowly realizes something is wrong on the feed, and escalates to full panic — while the astronaut (VIDEO A) cheerfully mishears every warning as praise. By the end Houston is stunned into silence.

## HOW THE TWO VIDEOS FIT TOGETHER

- Generate **VIDEO A** and **VIDEO B** separately, each as its own continuous keyframe-chained sequence (last held frame of each clip → start frame of the next, so the look stays consistent).
- **Correlation rule:** *Clip N here = Clip N in VIDEO A.* They are built to be intercut clip-by-clip; the conversation reads top-to-bottom across both files in lockstep.
- The `*(listens)*` / `*(beat)*` marks are **cut points** — that is where the astronaut's line plays on the other track. Hold a clear reaction (not a spoken line) on those beats so the editor has a clean frame to cut to.
- What he's reacting to is **the Mars feed on his monitor**, which mirrors the VIDEO A action of the same clip. Drive his expressions off that. The framing stays on HIM — the monitor is just the glow and the off-screen thing he's staring at.

# ACT 2 — THE CAPTURE (Clips 7–14)

## Clip 7 — The intruder appears
**Action:** Mid-smile, his eyes catch movement at the edge of the feed. The grin falters; he squints at the monitor and tilts his head.
**Dialogue:**
- *(listens, then narrows his eyes at the screen)*
- "Commander... is something moving behind you?"

## Clip 8 — The sneak
**Action:** He leans in close to the monitor, one hand rising halfway, trying to make out the grey shape creeping across the feed. Uneasy.
**Dialogue:**
- *(listens, distracted, staring past)*
- "No— there's a figure, there's a—"

**Action:** He half-rises out of his chair, gripping the edge of the console, urgent now, pointing at the screen.
**Dialogue:**

- *(listens, incredulous, mouth open)*

**Dialogue:**

**Dialogue:**

**Action:** He leans in close to the boom mic, slow and deliberate, over-enunciating, eyes wide and pleading.
**Dialogue:**
- *(listens, jaw tight)*

- *(silence — stares, says nothing)*

## Clip 20 — The punchline
**Action:** He slowly lowers the headset from his head and lets it hang, sinking back into the chair, eyes fixed on the screen with a thousand-yard stare. The image freezes on his hollow, speechless face.
**Dialogue:**
